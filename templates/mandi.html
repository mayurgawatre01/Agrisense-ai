💡 Bechne Se Pehle Yeh Tips Yaad Rakhein
  • Ek hi din mein 2-3 nazdeeki mandiyon ka rate compare karein.
  • Fasal ki quality (moisture, grading) jitni achi hogi, price utna behtar milega.
  • e-NAM portal par bhi apni fasal list karke dekhein — kabhi-kabhi behtar price milta hai.
  • Bumper harvest season mein price girta hai — agar storage available hai to thoda wait karna fayda de sakta hai.
  • District chunkar us district ki saari mandiyon (talukas) ka aaj ka rate ek saath compare kar sakte ho — best price wali mandi sabse upar dikhegi.
